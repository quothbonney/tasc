Frequently Asked Questions

Costs & Logistics

Is the camp really free? What about travel costs?

Yes, TASC is completely free! We cover everything including:

  • Travel to and from Tulsa
  • Housing and meals during the program
  • All program materials and activities
  • Cultural events and field trips

Do I need to bring anything special?

Just bring your everyday clothes, personal items, and enthusiasm! We'll provide:

  • All learning materials and equipment
  • A laptop for programming activities (if needed)
  • Lab safety gear for experiments
  • We'll send a detailed packing list after acceptance

Academic Questions

Is it too hard if I haven't taken advanced math?

Not at all! We welcome students of all academic backgrounds. Our program is designed to:

  • Meet you where you are and help you grow
  • Focus on understanding concepts, not memorizing formulas
  • Provide extra support when needed
  • Make learning fun and interactive

What if I'm not sure which track to choose?

Don't worry! You'll have a chance to explore all tracks during the first day before making your final choice. Our mentors will help you find the best fit for your interests.

Daily Life at TASC

Will I have free time or is it all work?

We believe in balance! Your day will include:

  • Fun group activities and social events
  • Cultural celebrations and community building
  • Free time to relax and make friends
  • Optional evening activities

What's a typical day like?

While each day is unique, here's a general schedule:

  • Morning: Interactive learning sessions
  • Afternoon: Hands-on projects and activities
  • Evening: Social events, cultural programs, or free time
  • Plenty of breaks and meal times to recharge
